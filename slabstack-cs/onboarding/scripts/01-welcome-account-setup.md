# Video 1: Welcome & Account Setup

**Series:** Slabstack Onboarding
**Runtime target:** 2:30–3:00
**Audience:** Brand new customers, first login
**Tools:** Loom (recording) · Descript (editing) · ElevenLabs (voiceover)

---

## On-Screen Cues Legend

- `[SHOW]` — display this screen or element
- `[CLICK]` — click this item
- `[ZOOM]` — zoom or highlight this area
- `[TYPE]` — keyboard input visible on screen
- `[CUT TO]` — transition to next screen

---

## Script

---

`[SHOW: Slabstack logo or branded title card]`

Welcome to Slabstack. This first video is going to get you logged in, oriented, and ready to go — in just a few minutes.

Let's start at the very beginning.

---

### Part 1: Logging In

`[SHOW: Example welcome email in an inbox]`

Before you can log in, check your inbox for a welcome email from your Customer Success Manager. That email confirms your account is ready and gives you everything you need to get started.

`[SHOW: Browser navigating to app.slabstack.com]`

Open a browser and go to **app.slabstack.com**.

`[CLICK: Log In button]`

Click **Log In**.

`[SHOW: Login screen with email and password fields]`
`[CLICK: "Forgot your password?" link]`

Since this is your first time, you won't have a password yet. Click **"Forgot your password?"** — this is how every new user gets access.

`[SHOW: Password reset screen]`
`[TYPE: email address into the email field]`

Enter the email address your CSM used to set up your account, then submit the form.

`[CUT TO: Example Auth0 password reset email in inbox]`

You'll get an email from Auth0 — that's the secure login service Slabstack uses. Open it and click the link to create your password.

`[SHOW: Password creation screen]`
`[TYPE: new password being entered]`

Set a strong password, confirm it, and save.

`[CUT TO: Browser back at app.slabstack.com]`
`[CLICK: Log In button]`
`[TYPE: email and password]`
`[CLICK: Submit / Sign In]`

Now head back to **app.slabstack.com**, log in with your email and new password, and you're in.

`[ZOOM: browser address bar or settings icon]`

One quick note: if you run into any trouble logging in, make sure your browser isn't blocking cookies. Slabstack requires cookies to keep your session active. You can check this in your browser's privacy settings.

---

### Part 2: Your Dashboard

`[SHOW: Slabstack dashboard — full screen]`

This is your Slabstack dashboard. It's your home base — a live snapshot of what's happening across your business: recent quotes, active projects, and key activity at a glance.

`[ZOOM: left-hand navigation bar]`

Everything in Slabstack lives in the left-hand navigation. This is how you move between sections — Quotes, Projects, Customers, and more. Click any item to go there. You can collapse this sidebar if you want more screen space.

`[CLICK: any section in the left nav — e.g., Quotes]`
`[SHOW: a list/grid view]`

When you're inside any section, your data displays in a grid — rows and columns, like a spreadsheet. This is how Slabstack shows your quotes, projects, and customers.

`[ZOOM: column headers and filter bar]`

You can click any column header to sort, and use the filter bar at the top to narrow things down — by date, status, customer, or whatever you need. Once you're working with real data, these filters will save you a lot of time.

---

### Part 3: Getting Help

`[ZOOM: chat bubble in the lower-right corner of the screen]`

If you ever have a question while you're in the app, click the chat bubble in the lower-right corner. That connects you directly to your support team for live help.

`[SHOW: text on screen — support@your-company.com]`

You can also reach us by email anytime at **support@your-company.com**. We're here to help.

---

`[SHOW: Slabstack dashboard or branded end card]`

That's it for Video 1. You're logged in and you know your way around.

In Video 2, we'll walk through creating your first quote — step by step, start to finish.

See you there.

---

## Production Notes

- Record at 1080p minimum
- Use a real or demo account with sample data visible in the grid — an empty account looks unfinished
- Pause 1 second after each `[CLICK]` to let the UI load visibly before continuing voiceover
- For the email steps: use a demo inbox — do not show a real customer or internal email
- Keep cursor movement slow and deliberate
- Use the same ElevenLabs voice across all 5 videos for series consistency
