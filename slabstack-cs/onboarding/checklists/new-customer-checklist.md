## Day 0 — Contract Signed

- [ ] Receive handoff from AE — discovery notes, pain points, custom commitments
- [ ] Run `/start-onboarding` to create the Asana project, Drive folder, Shortcut CSEng story, and Slack channel
- [ ] Send welcome email with kickoff meeting options (3 time slots within 5 business days)
- [ ] Add customer to the customer master sheet with `Health Score = green`
- [ ] Confirm primary contacts and titles on the customer side

---

## Week 1 — Kickoff & Account Setup

- [ ] Kickoff call held (45 min) — confirm goals, timeline, integrations, decision-makers
- [ ] Send kickoff recap email within 24 hours with agreed action items
- [ ] Provision customer account in Slabstack
- [ ] Send Video 1 (Welcome & Account Setup) to all internal users
- [ ] Confirm first user has logged in successfully
- [ ] Schedule Week 2 working session

**Red flags at end of Week 1:**
- No login activity → reach out same day
- Kickoff slipped past 5 business days → escalate to manager
- Custom commitments from AE not yet scoped → flag in Asana

---

## Week 2 — Configuration

- [ ] Configuration working session held
- [ ] Mix designs imported / entered
- [ ] Price list imported / entered
- [ ] Customer list imported (or migration plan agreed)
- [ ] User roles configured for all internal users
- [ ] Email templates set up
- [ ] Send Video 2 + 3 (Configurations + Activities)
- [ ] Confirm at least 3 users have logged in

---

## Week 3 — First Workflows

- [ ] First quote created end-to-end (with CSM on the call)
- [ ] First project created end-to-end
- [ ] First email sent from Slabstack to a real customer
- [ ] Send Video 4 (Creating a Quote)
- [ ] Working session: troubleshoot anything stuck

---

## Week 4 — Integration & Independence

- [ ] Dispatch integration tested (if in scope) — see `/integrations/sysdyne/README.md`
- [ ] Send Video 5 (Managing Projects)
- [ ] Verify customer is self-serving on quotes and projects (no CSM on the call)
- [ ] 30-day check-in scheduled
- [ ] Update customer master sheet with current `Health Score`
- [ ] Move Asana onboarding project to "30-day check-in" milestone

---

## End of Week 4 — Go/No-Go

Run `/go-live` to surface any open blockers across Asana, Shortcut, Intercom, and Gmail.

Decision criteria for marking onboarding complete:
- [ ] All 5 videos delivered
- [ ] All required configurations done
- [ ] At least 5 quotes and 5 projects created by the customer (not by CS)
- [ ] No P0/P1 Shortcut stories open against this customer
- [ ] No open onboarding blockers in Asana

If yes → run `/end-onboarding` to close out
If no → list specific blockers and target dates, escalate if dates slip
