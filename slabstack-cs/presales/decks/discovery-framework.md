# Discovery Framework

Use this on every first call with a prospective Slabstack customer. The goal is to surface enough to write a deal-specific demo, not to demo on the first call.

---

## Before the Call

- [ ] Confirm attendees and their roles
- [ ] Skim any prior emails or CRM notes from the AE
- [ ] Note the company's size, region, and dispatch system (if known)

---

## Opening (3 min)

- Thank them for the time, confirm the agenda
- Confirm how long they have (often less than the booked time)
- Set expectation: "I'll ask a lot of questions today — the goal is to make sure the demo I show you next time is built around your actual workflow."

---

## Current State (10 min)

1. Walk me through how a quote turns into a delivered job today.
2. Which systems are involved? (dispatch, accounting, email, spreadsheets)
3. Who touches the workflow? (estimators, dispatch, drivers, accounting)
4. Where does it break down most often?
5. What does a "good week" look like vs. a "bad week"?

---

## Pain Points (10 min)

1. If you could fix one thing about the quote-to-cash process tomorrow, what would it be?
2. How much time per week does your team lose to double entry between systems?
3. When a customer asks "where is my pour?" — how do you answer today?
4. What happens when the original quote needs to change after the job starts?

---

## Volume & Stakes (5 min)

1. How many quotes per week? Per month?
2. How many active projects at any one time?
3. Average ticket size? Largest job in the last year?
4. What's the cost of a missed pour or a billing error in your business?

---

## Decision Process (5 min)

1. Besides yourself, who else needs to be involved in this decision?
2. What's the timeline you're working against?
3. Have you evaluated other tools? What did you like / not like?
4. What would need to be true for you to say yes to Slabstack?

---

## Close (2 min)

- Recap the top 2–3 pain points back to them in their words
- Propose a tailored demo as the next step
- Confirm next attendees and a date
- Send a recap email same day with the agreed action items

---

## Post-Call

- [ ] Recap email sent within 24 hours
- [ ] Discovery notes logged in CRM
- [ ] Demo script tailored to their stated pain points
- [ ] AE notified of decision-makers, timeline, and competitive context
