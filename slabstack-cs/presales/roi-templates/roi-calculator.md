# ROI Calculator

Frame Slabstack's return on investment in numbers the buyer can defend internally. Use this template in the second or third call, after discovery has surfaced real volume data.

---

## Inputs to Gather in Discovery

| Input | Source | Example |
|---|---|---|
| Active projects per month | Customer-stated | 80 |
| Quotes sent per month | Customer-stated | 120 |
| Average time per quote today | Customer-stated | 25 minutes |
| Average time per project status update | Customer-stated | 10 minutes |
| Hourly fully-loaded cost per estimator | Customer or industry benchmark | $45/hr |
| Avg billing error per quarter | Customer-stated | 2 |
| Avg cost per billing error | Customer-stated | $1,200 |
| Avg lost-deal cost (missed follow-up) | Customer-stated | $8,000 |

## Calculation Framework

### Time Savings

| Activity | Time today | Time on Slabstack | Saved per item |
|---|---|---|---|
| Quote creation | 25 min | 5 min | 20 min |
| Project status updates | 10 min | 1 min | 9 min |
| Customer "where is my pour" calls | 8 min | 0 min (self-serve) | 8 min |
| Email log reconciliation | 15 min/day | 0 min | 15 min/day |

**Monthly time saved:**
`(quotes/month × 20 min) + (projects/month × 9 min × 4) + (status calls/month × 8 min) + (email reconciliation × 21 days × 15 min)`

**Dollarize:**
`time saved in hours × hourly cost`

## How to Present

1. **Don't lead with the number.** Walk through the inputs together so they buy into the math.
2. **Use their numbers, not yours.** If they push back on an input, lower it on the spot and rerun.
3. **Round down conservatively.** A 3x ROI you can defend beats a 10x ROI they don't believe.
4. **Send the math.** Email the filled-in calculator after the call so they can share it internally.

---

## What to Skip

- Don't include soft benefits (morale, brand image) in the headline number. Mention them, but don't dollarize them.
- Don't include savings from features that aren't shipping in the next 6 months.
- Don't include the time their existing tools cost — only the delta.
