## Discovery Framework (Quick Reference)

Key questions to ask on every first call:

1. **Current state** — How are you managing quotes and projects today?
2. **Pain points** — Where does the process break down?
3. **Volume** — How many quotes/projects per month?
4. **Team size** — How many people touch the workflow?
5. **Decision process** — Who else is involved in this decision?
6. **Timeline** — What's driving urgency?

## Handoff to CS

After a deal closes, the AE should pass to CS:

- [ ] Company overview and key contacts
- [ ] Pain points surfaced in discovery
- [ ] Any custom commitments made during sale
- [ ] Integration requirements
- [ ] Go-live timeline expectation
