# QBR Templates

Quarterly business review templates for every customer segment. Structured to demonstrate value, surface risks, and align on the next quarter.

---

## Templates

| File | Use Case |
|------|---------|
| `qbr-standard.md` | Standard QBR for all active customers |
| `qbr-at-risk.md` | Modified QBR for Yellow/Red health customers |
| `qbr-expansion.md` | QBR focused on upsell and expansion opportunity |

---

## Standard QBR Agenda (45 min)

| Section | Time | Owner |
|---------|------|-------|
| Welcome & agenda | 2 min | CSM |
| Quarter in review — usage and adoption | 10 min | CSM |
| Value realized — ROI and wins | 10 min | CSM + Customer |
| Open issues and resolutions | 5 min | CSM |
| Roadmap preview | 8 min | CSM |
| Next quarter goals | 8 min | Customer |
| Wrap up and next steps | 2 min | CSM |

---

## QBR Prep Checklist

Complete before every QBR:

- [ ] Pull usage data for the quarter
- [ ] Review open support tickets
- [ ] Note any product updates relevant to this customer
- [ ] Identify 2–3 specific wins to highlight
- [ ] Prepare renewal talking points if within 90 days
- [ ] Confirm attendees and send agenda 48 hours in advance

---

## After the QBR

Within 24 hours:
- [ ] Send recap email with action items
- [ ] Update customer health score in master tracker
- [ ] Log QBR date in customer master sheet
- [ ] Create follow-up tasks in Asana
