**For use in:** Executive Business Reviews (EBRs), annual account reviews, strategic planning conversations with multi-year customers.

**When to use:** Year 1 anniversary reviews, annual renewal conversations, accounts with strategic expansion potential, or any customer where a full-year retrospective is warranted.

Use the `/qbr` command to pull live data and generate the `.docx` version of this brief.

## Pre-EBR Checklist

- [ ] All year wins confirmed with evidence (no unverified claims)
- [ ] Support volume verified from Intercom
- [ ] Usage data verified from customer or internal systems
- [ ] Open items list complete and accurate
- [ ] Expansion signals confirmed before presenting
- [ ] Renewal context prepared if within 90 days
- [ ] Executive sponsor confirmed to attend
- [ ] Agenda sent 48–72h before the meeting
- [ ] Deck/materials prepared and reviewed internally
