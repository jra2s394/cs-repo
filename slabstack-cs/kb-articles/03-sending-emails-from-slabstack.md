# Sending Emails from Slabstack

Send quotes, updates, and one-off messages to customers without leaving the project.

## Overview

Every email you send from Slabstack is automatically logged against the project's communication thread. That means anyone on your team can pick up where you left off — no more forwarding email chains or losing context when someone is out.

## Step-by-Step

1. Open the project you want to email about.
2. Click the **Communication** tab.
3. Click **+ New Email**.
4. Select the recipient from the project's contacts list, or type a new email address.
5. Pick a template (Quote Sent, Delivery Confirmation, Follow-Up) or start from blank.
6. Edit the subject and body. Slabstack inserts the customer name, project name, and quote number automatically if you use placeholder tokens like `{{customer_name}}`.
7. Attach files from the project's **Files** tab, or upload from your computer.
8. Click **Send**.
9. Confirm the email appears in the Communication thread with a green "Sent" indicator.

## Tips

- If the customer replies, their reply lands back in the Communication thread automatically — no need to forward.
- Set up a signature once under **Settings → Profile** and it applies to every email you send.
- For emails you send often (delivery confirmations, post-pour follow-ups), build a template under **Settings → Email Templates** to save time.
