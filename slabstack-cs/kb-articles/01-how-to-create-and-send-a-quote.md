# How to Create and Send a Quote

Build a quote in Slabstack and send it to your customer in under five minutes.

## Overview

Quotes in Slabstack are tied to a project and a customer. Once you create the quote, you can email it directly from the platform — the customer gets a clean, branded PDF, and the activity is logged against the project automatically.

## Step-by-Step

1. From the dashboard, click **Quotes → New Quote**.
2. Select an existing customer, or click **+ New Customer** if this is a first-time prospect.
3. Choose the project this quote belongs to. If no project exists yet, create one from the dropdown.
4. Add line items. For each line: select the mix design, enter quantity (cubic yards or meters), and confirm the unit price. Slabstack pulls your current price list automatically.
5. Add any adjustments — delivery fees, escalators, discounts — under **Adjustments**.
6. Review the totals on the right-hand panel.
7. Click **Save Draft** if you need to come back to it, or **Send Quote** to email it now.
8. On the send screen, confirm the recipient email and add a short message. The quote PDF attaches automatically.
9. Click **Send**.

## Tips

- Save a frequently used mix and quantity combo as a **Quote Template** to skip steps 4–5 on repeat business.
- The status badge on the Quotes list (Draft, Sent, Viewed, Accepted) updates in real time — no need to refresh.
- If a customer asks for a revised quote, click **Duplicate** instead of editing the original. It preserves the audit trail.
