## Article Format (Intercom Standard)

Each article follows this structure for maximum search visibility and self-resolution rate:

```
Title (matches how users search)
Summary sentence (shown in Intercom search preview)

## Overview
One paragraph — what this article covers

## Step-by-Step
Numbered steps, one action per step

## Tips
2–3 pro tips from real CS experience

## Related Articles
Links to 2–3 related KB articles
```
